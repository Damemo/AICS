Who We Are

AICS is specialised in providing support to participants with NDIS, Veteran card holders and Home care packages. AICS also provides it's quality services to many other organisations that supports carers, seniors, people with disability and multicultural community.

We are a trusted, reputable and multi-award organisation that's primary purpose is to improve the well being of individuals through a more personalized care services. At AICS majority of our profit is invested back in the community in projects that support our carers, people with additional needs, seniors and those in need.
